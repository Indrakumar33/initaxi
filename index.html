About CityRide

Founded in 2015, CityRide has grown to become one of the most trusted cab service providers in the region. We started with just three vehicles and a passion for excellent customer service.

Today, we operate a fleet of over 200 vehicles serving thousands of satisfied customers every month. Our drivers are carefully vetted professionals who prioritize your safety and comfort.

We're committed to reducing our environmental impact and have recently added hybrid and electric vehicles to our fleet.
